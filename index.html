Hey UXer, we can‘t wait to welcome you to our UXSoc family! This is a short primer listing the available officer positions and their accompanying duties for the AY 2019-2020. Interested? Don‘t forget to apply!

📝 Design & Consultancy

We are the core of UXSoc that advocates the practice of user experience. We handle all client projects in and outside of Ateneo. As officers of this department, you will focus on making products to be both functional and impactful for our clients.

Product Designer

If you’re interested in creating tangible products, then this is the perfect role for you. You design products for the user and continuously seek to improve them. The specializations in this position are:

  • • Visual Designer: Focuses on the “look” of the product and creates wireframes, mockups, MVPs, and design elements
  • • Interaction Designer: Focuses on the “feel” of the product and crafts the experiences of the user in motion, action, and reaction components

Content Strategist

If you want to learn how to read minds (or close enough), content strategy is your field. You carry out user research and strategize the best way to present the client’s content in their product. The specializations in this position are:

  • • Researcher: Conducts stakeholder interviews for user research, defines user problems, maps out user journeys and empathy maps
  • • Information Architecture Officer: Prepares site maps and utilizes other appropriate UX strategies with clients as well as other members of the Design and Consultancy team

💻 Engineering

Do you want to turn ideas into reality? Working hand in hand with UXDC, Engineering works on transforming design masterpieces into user-centric interfaces. You'll get the chance to build website projects of UXSoc and its clients. There are two Engineering divisions: Front-End Division and the Back-End Division.

Associate Front-End Developer

  • • Develops and implements design interfaces of both UXSoc and client projects
  • • Ensures that websites are accessible and responsive

Associate Back-End Developer

  • • Creates servers and maintains databases for both UXSoc and client projects
  • • Interested in building digitized systems that cater to big data

😊 Human Resources

UXSoc is not just about a client’s user experience, but also our own member’s user experience. Human Resources focuses on organizing events and initiatives that help the members to grow and bond with one another.

Member Happiness

  • • Organizes Bonding Activities and Team-building
  • • Conducts ICs with other officers and members
  • • Opportunity widening: Inviting members to UX events outside of UXSoc

Member Growth

  • • Organizes the HR Curriculum of articles and FB group posts
  • • Conducts research on the current composition of members
  • • Assesses trends and and creates reports from event and IC responses/feedback
  • • Conducts SWOT Analysis on events in egress and from feedback and presents finding

🎉 Events

At UXSoc’s core, we want to nurture passionate designers and what better way to inspire aspiring designers than through our events? This department focuses on organizing informative and meaningful events.

Event Manager

  • • Oversees the whole event and coordinates with other departments
  • • Compiles all inputs from other sub-event departments and organizes processing sessions
  • • Leads an event with the help of the Events VP and ensure its overall success by meeting the event’s main objectives

Programs and Logistics

  • • Creates engaging program flows and prepares effective contingency plans that include worst, and best scenarios for every event
  • • Reserves the perfect venue for the events and coordinates with external suppliers (OSA, food supplier, tokens, certificates, etc.)

Audience and Speaker Experience

  • • Ensures that speakers and guests have a stress-free, memorable and enjoyable experience possible
  • • Creates evaluation forms for the audience to answer and collates the data to be used for future events

🎨 Branding

Looks matter, and Branding knows it. Branding focuses on designing aesthetically pleasing and effective collaterals for UXSoc’s various events, initiatives, and projects. Officers will have the opportunity to learn and grow their skills in design theory, branding, illustration, and use of Adobe illustrator/photoshop.

Design Lead

  • • Leads a small design team in the planning and creating of collaterals and social media campaigns to promote the org’s events, projects, and initiatives
  • • Decides the design direction and aesthetic of the collaterals.
  • • In charge of editing, typography, and layout design
  • • Ensures that all visual output of the org follows the org‘s branding guidelines
  • • Works with copywriters (from Content Marketing) to ensure that the content being put out is appropriate

Illustrator

  • • Creates illustrations for articles and feature pieces
  • • May be requested to create illustrations for UXDC projects and for event collaterals

Videographer

  • • Takes video documentation and edits final video outputs for UXSoc’s events (will be expected to be able to attend events)
  • • Ensures quality control over all video output of the org
  • • May be requested to create video content for event collaterals

Photographer

  • • Takes photo documentation and edits final photo output for UXSoc’s events (will be expected to be able to attend events)
  • • Ensures quality control over all photos that UXSoc releases
  • • May be requested to take photos for event collaterals

📝 Content Marketing

Looks matter, but so does what’s on the inside. Content Marketing works closely with Branding to produce original, engaging content for our online platforms. In their written pieces on UX and our own initiatives and projects, this department displays what UXSoc is all about.

Editor

  • • Reviews and revises content for consumption via social media.
  • • Produces educational pieces on the basics of User Experience and perspectives of Filipinos working in the Philippines’ User Experience field
  • • Produces articles and feature pieces covering User Experience Society’s initiatives and projects

Copywriter

  • • Produces marketing content regularly released for each UXSoc’s initiative (ie. Marketing spiels)
  • • Works together with branding and events to determine the information required for marketing content
  • • Produces humorous feature pieces theme on appropriate holidays and periods
  • • Produces articles and feature pieces covering UXSoc’s initiatives and projects

💸 Finance

Money makes the world go round. If you want to learn more about the crucial role cash plays in our organization, Finance is your go to. This department focuses on increasing financial literacy and maximizing our funds. As we are compensated for our client work, these officers will also be able to experience the real-life financial processes of a design firm.

Finance Officer

  • • Plans and executes the foundation of UXSoc’s administrative systems for finance
  • • Proposes and implements fundraising events for the organization (from ideation, production, to selling)
  • • Looks for possible tie-ups and sponsorships with companies suitable for UXSoc initiatives and events
  • • Creates content and events on Financial Literacy to help better equip UXSoc members into becoming actual design professionals in the future (may include sharing and posting online articles and hosting events)
